Homepage

A natural disaster is a sudden, extreme event caused by natural phenomena that can cause great loss of life or destruction to the environment, infrastructure, or private property. Another common name for them is natural hazards. This website covers twelve different natural disasters, but these aren't all of them. There are still plenty of others like limnic eruptions, droughts, heat waves, and comets that this website doesn't go over. Learn some neat facts, and some cool safety tips that may just save your life one day!
